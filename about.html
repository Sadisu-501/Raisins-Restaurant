About us

At Raisins Restaurant, Bakery & More, we believe great food brings people together. From sunrise to sunset, our kitchen and ovens are hard at work crafting delicious meals, fresh-baked goods, and comforting classics that feel like home. Whether you're stopping by for a hearty breakfast, a leisurely lunch, or a sweet treat to brighten your day, Raisins is your neighborhood destination for flavor, warmth, and community.
